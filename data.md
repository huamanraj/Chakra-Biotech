

Chakra Biotech LLP: Saffron Agri-Technology 

## About the Company d

**Chakra Biotech LLP** is an emerging Agri + Technology and Aeroponics based company in **Jaipur, Rajasthan**, focused on indoor aeroponic saffron cultivation.

The company aims to democratize saffron farming beyond Kashmir by using advanced climate-controlled systems and modern cultivation technologies.

---

## Vision & Mission

### Vision

To build India's leading indoor saffron cultivation ecosystem, enabling high-quality "Red Gold" production in non-traditional regions through technology, self-reliance, and sustainable agri-innovation.

### Mission

To empower farmers, agri-entrepreneurs, and institutions by introducing aeroponic and hydroponic saffron farming using controlled environments that replicate Kashmir-like conditions, creating profitable, scalable, and climate-resilient agriculture models in Rajasthan.

---

## Problem Statement (Pain Points)

The current state of Saffron cultivation faces the following challenges:

* 
**Geographical Limitation:** Cultivation in India is geographically limited and climate-dependent .


* 
**Condition Requirements:** Traditional farming requires specific cold conditions unavailable in most regions.


* 
**Import Dependency:** High dependency on imports and limited domestic production.


* 
**Farmer Access:** Farmers in arid regions lack access to high-value crop opportunities.


* 
**Inefficiency:** Existing cultivation methods are low-yield and resource-intensive.



---

## Our Solution

Chakra Biotech LLP addresses these issues by:

1. Using **aeroponic indoor farming systems**.


2. Creating **controlled environment agriculture (CEA)** setups.


3. 
**Replicating Kashmir-like** temperature, humidity, and light conditions.


4. Enabling **year-round saffron cultivation**.


5. Making saffron farming **viable in hot desert regions** like Rajasthan.



---

## Product / Technology Overview

The core technology focuses on outcome, quality, and scalability rather than hardware complexity. Key features include:

1. 
**Soil-less Cultivation:** Aeroponic saffron cultivation without soil .


2. 
**Precision Control:** Management of temperature, humidity, lighting, and  levels.


3. 
**Higher Yield:** significantly higher yield per square foot compared to traditional farming.


4. 
**Resource Efficiency:** Reduced water usage and optimized nutrient delivery .


5. 
**Quality Assurance:** Laboratory-tested, premium-quality saffron output .
